# Scientific Clinical Compliance

AMA mobile plugin category for clinical research documentation, clinical reporting, treatment-plan structure, and ISO 13485 quality-system documentation.

This pack is documentation-only in version `0.1.0`.

- Installs four related skills as one category.
- Preserves upstream reference documents and templates.
- Does not ship Python, shell, Node, PowerShell, batch, or desktop helper scripts.
- Does not provide diagnosis, prescription, or autonomous clinical decision-making.
- Future validation, de-identification, and document-generation helpers must be implemented as AMA Swift host intents before they are exposed as executable actions.

## Skills

- `clinical-decision-support`: group-level clinical research evidence synthesis and decision-algorithm drafting.
- `clinical-reports`: de-identified report structure for case reports, diagnostic reports, clinical trial reports, and patient documentation.
- `treatment-plans`: clinician-reviewed treatment-plan structure, goals, monitoring, and documentation checklists.
- `iso-13485-certification`: medical-device QMS documentation gap analysis and template guidance.

## Mobile Boundaries

Use this pack to draft, review, and structure documentation. Do not use it as a substitute for licensed clinical judgment, emergency care, legal regulatory advice, or final audit certification.
