# Direction Output Contract

Return the product-direction result directly in chat.

## Output shape

- `Direction`
- `Demand evidence`
- `Main constraint`
- `Recommended approach`
- `Open question`
- `Next step`

## Rules

- Do not write repo-local documents by default.
- Do not create approval states or document lineage.
- Keep the result short, decision-oriented, and grounded in observed evidence.
